Hi, I'm Benji!

I am a game developer working at GoldFire Studios.

In my free time, I make small games, toys and music tools that you can find on itch.io.

The code for these is open source and hosted on Github.

I also compose music for video games, commercials and short films. You can hear some of that on Soundcloud.

I've released a couple of pop albums which you can listen to on Apple Music or Spotify.

I've produced a few JavaScript tutorials for YouTube, and sometimes I write about JavaScript on Medium.

I also recently started a progressive political channel on YouTube.

If you want, you can follow me on Twitter, where sometimes I post art. I also post colorful photos to Instagram.

Oh, and I have a profile on LinkedIn.
